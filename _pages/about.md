I am Elizabeth Cornick [(she/her)](https://studentaffairs.howard.edu/diversity-inclusion/lgbtq-resources/pronouns), a Ph.D. student in English and American Studies Fellow at the University of Miami. My research investigates how geography and nostalgia influence narrative aesthetics of the American novel in the 1960s.

I regularly update this website with all my [publications](https://elizabethcornick.github.io/publications/), [projects](https://elizabethcornick.github.io/projects/), [conference presentations](https://elizabethcornick.github.io/talks/), and my [curriculum vitae](https://elizabethcornick.github.io/assets/cv_elizabeth_cornick.pdf).
